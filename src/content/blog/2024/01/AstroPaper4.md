# AstroPaper 4.0

[AstroPaper 4.0 was released](https://astro-paper.pages.dev/posts/astro-paper-v4/) and the author did more than just
upgrade the package to Astro v4.

I'm sure glad I made a post detailing everything I did to get AstroPaper 3.0 working. It turned out to be very
useful in quickly setting up this new version. 😅 If I were a true master I would have merged the new version in or
something, but since this blog is so young I just cut over the changes and the content and called it a day.

You might not notice many visual changes to the site, but under the hood, we are running on v4.

Thanks to [Sat Naing](https://github.com/satnaing) and all of the contributors for the hard work!
